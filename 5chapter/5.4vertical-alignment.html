PIAIC stands for the "Presidential Initiative for Artificial Intelligence and Computing," which is an initiative launched by the Government of Pakistan with the aim to develop and enhance Pakistan's potential in the field of artificial intelligence and computing. The initiative offers a range of programs, including online courses and certifications, to help individuals learn and develop skills in various areas of AI and computing.

The PIAIC program aims to create a skilled workforce that can meet the growing demand for AI and computing professionals in Pakistan and around the world. The program focuses on teaching emerging technologies such as AI, machine learning, data science, cloud computing, and blockchain, among others.

The PIAIC program has gained significant popularity in Pakistan, and thousands of individuals have enrolled in its various courses and programs. The program has also received support and recognition from various industry leaders, including Google and IBM, among others.
