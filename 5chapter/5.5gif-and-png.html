Images

There are two types of images

  1. Bitmap Images
    2. bitmap images are made up of pixels and are resolution dependent. Such as jpeg, png, gif

    On web pages their resolution should be 72pixel per inches. Printed material(books, magazines etc) images resolution should be 300 dots per inches.
