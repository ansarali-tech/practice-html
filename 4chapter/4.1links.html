Links Types

There are 3 types of links

  1. Links to other sites
  2. Links to pages of same site
  3. link to specific part of same page

Links to Other Sites

Links to other sites require absolute URL that contain domain name.

  • facebook
  • twitter
  • instagram
  • youtube

Links to Pages of Same Site

To link to the different pages of same webesite require relative URL without domain name. It is used whenever you are linking pages to your own webesite.
